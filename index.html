About Me

Lucy McGuigan believes in the power of design as a conduit for communicating the complex ideas that drive us. Originally from New Jersey, she now lives and works in Dallas, TX. Lucy received a Master of Arts in Art History from Southern Methodist University in 2016. She earned her Bachelor of Arts in Sociology from the University of Pennsylvania in 2011.

With a professional background that has spanned from teaching high school math to grant writing for a nonprofit community design center—and many other roles in between—she is excited to bring her diverse frames of reference to the task of building beautiful, functional websites.
